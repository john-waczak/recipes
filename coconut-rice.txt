COCONUT RICE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  White rice              2 cup(s)
  Coconut milk            1 can(s)
  Water                   1.5 cup(s)
  sugar                   3 tablespoon(s)
  salt                    1 tablespoon(s)
  cinnamon                (optional) 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  In a sieve, wash rice thoroughly to remove some of the starch. Add all ingredients except for rice to a saucepan and bring to a boil, stirring until everything is incorporated. Add rice to pan and give a good stir. Cover sauce pan with lid and reduce to a simmer. Let sit for 30-45 minutes or until rice is cooked completely. You should see a thin film of coconut milk at the top. Do not stir while the rice is cooking! Fluff with a fork and then serve.
