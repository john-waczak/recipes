Carne Asada marinade
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Tri-tip               (get from Costco)
cilantro (chopped)    1 cup
Tampico (orange)      1 cup
Garlic paste          1 tablespoon(s)
Lime juice            3 limes
vinegar               1/2 cup 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Mix ingredients and place in a large ziplock bag with the meat. Marinade 24 hr. Cook on a hot grill turning frequently and basting with butter/garlic mixture. Take off of grill when internal temperature reaches ~120 F. Pull off of grill and tent in aluminum foil for 10 minutes to allow to rest. Cut against the grain.
