CHICKEN AND DUMPLINGS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  milk                        1.5 cup(s)
  frozen peas and carrots     1 cup(s)
  choped cooked chicken       1 cup(s)
  condensed cream of chicken  1 can(s)
  Bisquick                    1 cup(s)
  milk (for dumplings)        1/3 cup(s)
  Paprika (optional) 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Directions
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
In a large saucepan, add the 1.5 cups milk, peas, carrots, chicken, and soup. Heat to a boil over high heat, stirring frequently. Reduce to a simmer.

In a medium bowl, mix Bisquick and 1/3 cup of milk until a soft dough forms. Gently drop spoonfuls into the chiken mixture. Do not submerge dumplings in the soup. Sprinkle with paprika.

Simmer on low heat for 10 more minutes or longer until dumplings are cooked through.
